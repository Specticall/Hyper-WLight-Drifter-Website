The Barren Hills

Difficulty

The Barren Hills or simply Wastes is the South region of the land of light. The Barren Hills is home to a large underground laboratory complex that also serves as a robot factory and is responsible for thecreation of various creatures seen around the world such as the Dirks and blue-skins. Scattered about the desert are a number of ruined mechanical structures resembling pieces of a large spacecraft possibly also created in the labs.
